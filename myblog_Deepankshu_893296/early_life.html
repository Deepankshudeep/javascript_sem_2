Schooling at Spring Dale Senior School

During my early life, I attended Spring Dale Senior School. I was part of a joint family, which was a wonderful experience. I was known for being a sharp-minded kid in school, actively participating in mathematics and science Olympiads. Moreover, I also enjoyed participating in fancy dress competitions, which were a lot of fun.

Curiosity about Space and Astronomy

From a young age, I had a deep curiosity about space and astronomy. I loved stargazing and learning about the stars, planets, and the universe. This fascination with space eventually led me to explore more about the field of astrology.

High School at Sidana International School

For my high school, I continued my education at Sidana International School. It was here that I further nurtured my interests and continued my academic journey.
